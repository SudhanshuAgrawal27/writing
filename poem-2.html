Camera

I tried to focus on my destiny, After all, it was down a straight road in front of me. But every time I looked, It looked a different kind of blurry. I zoomed in my camera and tapped the screen. A scene snapped sharply into focus, But only momentarily. Long enough to know it was there, Short enough to confuse me. I tapped again, a different picture, Again, a different color, Again, a different texture, Again, a different future, Again, a different me.

— Sudhanshu
